What are Semantic Elements?

A semantic element defines its meaning to both the browser and the developer. Semantic elements are elements with a meaning. Semantic is the study of the meanings of words and phrases in a language. Semantic elements defines its content. Compared to non-semantic element which provide no definition and tell nothing about its content. HTML5* semantic elements are supported in all modern browsers and read older browsers how to handle unknown elements.
